Income taxes in New York

     New York imposes state income taxes that range from 4% to 8.82%, with additional local taxes in some areas. The Big Apple, known for its iconic skyline, also comes with a hefty city income tax ranging from 3.078% to 3.876%. While these rates might seem steep, they fund essential services and infrastructure, contributing to the vibrant culture and opportunities that make New York a hub for business and innovation.
